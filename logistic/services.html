Dangerous Goods (DG)

Dangerous Goods (DG) are substances or articles that are hazardous due to their sensitivity to air temperature, pressure, and vibration. They can pose risks to human and animal health, disrupt aviation safety when transported by air, and potentially damage transportation equipment. Dangerous Goods (DG) can be transported by air and other transport vehicles, provided they comply with requirements, rules, and procedures for handling DG according to their type/classification.

PT Raven International Logistics accepts shipments of dangerous goods, adhering to applicable regulations including IATA Dangerous Goods Regulations standards, National AVSEC Regulations, and policies established by respective shipping operators.
