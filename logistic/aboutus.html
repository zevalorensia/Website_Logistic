About Us

Company Profile

PT Raven International Logistics was founded by Michael with a vision to transform the logistics industry, particularly in the management of dangerous goods. Stemming from his personal experiences, Michael aimed to provide innovative and safe logistics solutions. From the beginning, the company has prioritized safety, regulatory compliance, and superior customer service. With proactive technology and human resource development, PT Raven continually strives to be the top choice for global dangerous goods shipping.

Our Vision

"Our vision is to become a leading global logistics company by providing innovative, efficient, and reliable logistics solutions for our customers."

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver high-quality logistics services that meet our customers' needs with timeliness, safety, and efficiency, while continuously innovating and adapting to changes in the global market.
